1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and a hashed version of your password. We never store your password in plain text. If you sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive your name, email, and profile picture from those providers.

When you use the app, we store your personal data (tasks, habits, journal entries, expenses, etc.) in MongoDB Atlas, linked to your account. This data is private to you.

With your browser's permission, we may save your approximate geolocation to provide weather information and for security purposes. Only site administrators can view location data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the SavahLifestyle service
  • To authenticate your identity and keep your account secure
  • To personalize your dashboard and greetings
  • To send you notifications you have opted into
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve features (aggregated, anonymized)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be shared with:

  • MongoDB Atlas — our database provider (data processor)
  • Vercel — our hosting provider (infrastructure)
  • Google / Facebook — only if you use social sign-in
  • Law enforcement if legally required

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including JWT authentication, bcrypt password hashing (12 rounds), HTTPS encryption in transit, and MongoDB Atlas security features including IP allowlisting and encrypted storage.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us via the Contact page. You can also export your data by contacting support.

6. Cookies

We use localStorage (not traditional cookies) to store your session token, theme preference, and cached settings locally in your browser. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

7. Children's Privacy

SavahLifestyle is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via the notification system.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, visit our Contact page.
