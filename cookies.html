SavahLifestyle uses localStorage (browser storage) rather than traditional cookies. Here's what we store and why.

What We Store

  • sl:token — Your JWT authentication token to keep you logged in
  • sl:user — Your cached profile data (name, email, role)
  • sl:theme — Your dark/light mode preference
  • sl:notifs — Your notification history
  • sl:* — Various app data (todos, habits, etc.) cached locally for speed

Third-Party Storage

If you sign in with Google or Facebook, those services may set their own cookies per their respective privacy policies.

How to Clear

You can clear all stored data by going to your browser's developer tools → Application → Local Storage and clearing all entries for this site, or by signing out.
